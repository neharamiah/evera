About Us

Evera is a Sustainability-first platform focused on Transforming the Future of Logistics. We help businesses Track, Visualise, and Analyze their Carbon Emissions within Supply Chains - combining Innovation and Technology






Our Mission

Our Mission is to make sustainability measurable, accessible and actionable for every supply chain. Through data driven insights and dashboards, we empower companies toreduce their environmental impact and pave the way towards net zero logistics.
